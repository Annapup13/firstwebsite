America Chavez

In the MCU(Marvel Cinematic Universe) America has only appeared in one film, Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. Although she is in less projects than most other characters, she compensates by making her time on screen meaningful. Played by Xochitl Gomez , America has the abiliity to travel through universes which is pretty awesome. In the comics she is also a member of the Young Avengers alongside Ms. Marvel, Loki, and few others.
