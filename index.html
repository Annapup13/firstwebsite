The Best Marvel Characters Potrayed In The MCU

In My Opinion

  • America Chavez
  • Kamala Khan
  • Loki Laufeyson
  • Marc Spector (and alters)
  • Wanda Maximoff

America Chavez

In the MCU(Marvel Cinematic Universe) America has only appeared in one film, Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. Although she is in less projects than most other characters, she compensates by making her time on screen meaningful. Played by Xochitl Gomez , America has the abiliity to travel through universes which is pretty awesome. In the comics she is also a member of the Young Avengers alongside Ms. Marvel, Loki, and few others.

Kamala Khan

In the MCU, Kamala is every MCU fan. She is obsessed with the Avengers but most especially Captain Marvel. In her tv show Ms. Marvel she gains her powers through a bangle gifted to her by her grandmother. She will be seen again in The Marvels.

Loki Laufeyson

Played by Tom Hiddleston, Loki is an amazing and complex character. The first project he was in was Thor and his latest was in his own show.

Wanda Maximoff

Elizabeth Olsen plays a powrful witch with the power to quite litterally rewrite reality and all she chose to do was find a world where she can be happy. The thousands of dead bodies she left in her wake were, however, problematic. Wanda Maximoff's first apperance in the MCU was in 2014 in a post credit scene featured in Captain America the Winter Soldier. She has been featured in many other projects with her most recent being Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness.
